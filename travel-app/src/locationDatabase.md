**Description:** Snagov is a scenic commune built around Lake Snagov, the largest natural lake in Romania (600 ha, 16 km long). Set amid old oak forests, it is a historic spa and resort town. The area is famous as the legendary burial site of Vlad the Impaler (Dracula).

**Top Attractions:**
- 🏛️ **Snagov Monastery** — 14th-century island monastery, legendary tomb of Vlad the Impaler. Reachable by boat.
- 🏰 **Snagov Palace** — Former royal palace built by Prince Nicholas of Romania in 1928, on the shores of Lake Snagov.
- 🌲 **Snagov Natural Complex Reserve** — 1,147 ha nature reserve including Snagov Lake and Snagov Forest.
- 🚴 **Snagov Eco-Tourism Center** — Bike rentals, kayaks, audio-guided boat tours, 5 cycling circuits.
- 🎭 **Snagov Museum** — Collections relating to 130 personalities connected to Snagov, opened 2008.

### 🌍 About Sinaia

**Description:** Known as the "Pearl of the Carpathians," Sinaia is a royal mountain resort town nestled in the Bucegi Mountains.

**Top Attractions:**
- 🏰 **Peleș Castle** — Neo-Renaissance royal palace, one of Romania's most spectacular sights. Tripadvisor #1.
- 🏯 **Pelișor Castle** — Smaller royal retreat in Art Nouveau style, built for King Ferdinand I and Queen Marie.
- ⛪ **Sinaia Monastery** — Founded 1695, the namesake of the town.
- 🎰 **Sinaia Casino** — Historic 1913 Belle Époque building.
- 🚡 **Bucegi Cable Car** — Connects the resort with Cota 1,400 and Cota 2,000 mountain platforms.
- 🏔️ **Bucegi Natural Park** — 326 km² of protected mountain terrain.
- 🎻 **George Enescu Memorial House** — Villa of Romania's greatest composer.

### 🌍 About Brașov

**Top Attractions:**

- 🏛️ **Council Square (Piața Sfatului)** — The stunning medieval heart of the old town.
- 🗼 **Catherine's Gate** — The only surviving original medieval city gate.
- 🏔️ **Tampa Mountain** — 900m peak with cable car and panoramic views.
- 🏰 **Bran Castle** — "Dracula's Castle," 30 min from Brașov.
- ⛷️ **Poiana Brașov** — Top ski resort, 12 km from city center.
- 🏯 **Râșnov Fortress** — Restored medieval peasant fortress.

### 🌍 About Curtea de Argeș

**Description:** One of the oldest towns in Wallachia, former medieval capital. Royal Necropolis holds tombs of Kings Carol I, Ferdinand I, Queen Marie, and King Michael I.

**Top Attractions:**
- ⛪ **Curtea de Argeș Cathedral & Monastery** — Romania's most important Orthodox site and Royal Necropolis.
- 🏛️ **Princely Court (Curtea Domnească)** — Ruins of the medieval Wallachian royal palace.
- 💧 **Vidraru Dam** — One of the largest dams in Romania, 30 km away.
- 🛣️ **Transfăgărășan Highway** — Iconic mountain road begins near here. Tripadvisor #1 in the area.
- 🏰 **Poienari Fortress** — The real castle of Vlad the Impaler, accessible via 1,480 steps.
- 🌊 **Bâlea Lake & Waterfall** — High-altitude glacial lake via the Transfăgărășan.
- 🎨 **Museum of Art & Argeș County Museum** — Local history and cultural heritage.
